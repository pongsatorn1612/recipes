Yum MicroSalmon

1. Make the Yum sauce by add chopped red onion, white onion,
Thai red chili, and garic into mix of water, cane sugar, and
fish sauce.
2. Take a piece of Salmon put into microwave 900 watt for 3 minutes
And then flip Salmon to the other side then heat with microwave 900 
watt for another 3 minutes.
3. Pull The Yum sauce all over the Salmon
4. Finally EAT!!

Note: In step 2 you can add seasame oil, enchance the aroma of the
Salmon... Yummy ;)
